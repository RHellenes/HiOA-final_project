In emergencies, relevant information might be hard to find. Lives might be at stake, and it can be difficult to keep calm in stressful situations. 
Simple tasks like navigating to information on your smartphone, can be extremely demanding under pressure. 
During such events time is of an essence, and those precious minutes we spend to find information should rather be used on staying safe and helping others.
Today’s norwegian solution consists of the civil defense sirens(tornado siren) and mass media, and these are the only official tools for alerting the citizens in a crisis. 
As of today, there is no digital solution that communicates the message directly to the public in Norway.
